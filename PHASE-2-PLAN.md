## Feature Details

### 1. Analytics — Website, Vendor & Enquiry Tracking
- **Website analytics:** visitors, page views, top pages, traffic sources.
- **Vendor analytics:** per-vendor profile views, enquiries received, contact clicks — visible to the vendor in their dashboard.
- **Enquiry tracking:** full log of who enquired, which vendor, when, and status — so leads are never lost.

> _Why it matters:_ vendors get a tangible reason to stay (and upgrade), and the client sees platform health at a glance.

### 2. Reviews & Comments
- Star ratings and written reviews on every vendor profile.
- Optional moderation before a review goes live.
- Comments to let vendors respond and build relationships.

> _Why it matters:_ reviews are the single biggest trust signal for booking event vendors.

### 3. Geolocation Search
- "Vendors near me" and search/filter by city, area, or distance.
- Map view of vendors by location.

> _Why it matters:_ event vendors are chosen locally — this makes discovery effortless.

### 4. Referral Programme
- Referral links/codes for vendors and clients.
- Track referrals and reward successful sign-ups or bookings.

> _Why it matters:_ low-cost, organic growth driven by the community itself.

### 5. Owambe Event Page & Registry ⭐
The centrepiece of Phase 2. Hosts create a **personal event page** they can share with guests, and every page doubles as **free advertising for the vendors involved**.

**MVP structure of an event page:**

**Cover Section**
- Event Name
- Host Name(s)
- Cover Photo
- Event Date
- Event Location
- Upload e-card

> _Example:_ **ChiChi's Naming Ceremony** — Saturday, 12th June 2027 — Manchester, UK

**Welcome Message**
A short, warm note from the hosts to guests.

**About the Event**
A simple section where hosts explain what the event is, why it's special, and what guests should expect. Works well for:
- Traditional weddings
- Engagement ceremonies
- Naming ceremonies
- Milestone birthdays

**Event Information**
- 📍 Venue
- 🕒 Time
- 👗 Dress Code
- 🚗 Parking Information
- 🏨 Accommodation Recommendations

**Dietary Information**
Genuinely useful for guests. e.g. _"Food served will include: …"_ or _"Please notify us of any dietary requirements."_

**Photo & Video Gallery**
Engagement photos, pre-event shoots, family photos — makes the page feel personal.

**Gift Registry Section** _(the key section)_
- **Cash Gifts** — guests contribute towards a honeymoon, new home, baby fund, or birthday celebration.
- **Gift Wishlist** — hosts list their preferred gifts.

**RSVP (Lightweight)**
Not full RSVP management — just:
- ✅ Attending
- ❌ Unable to attend

Optionally collect: Name, Number of guests.

**Vendor Showcase**
"Our Vendors" — each linked back to their vendor profile:
- Photography by: _ABC Photography_
- Cake by: _XYZ Cakes_
- Decor by: _Luxury Events_

> _Why it matters:_ **every event page becomes free advertising for the vendors** — a built-in growth loop unique to Owambe Connect.

### 10. Homepage Vendor Spotlight

> _Why it matters:_ a premium, monetisable spot that vendors will pay to occupy.

## Accounts & Sign-In Model

- **To create an event page**, a client must create a **client account** so they can manage and edit their page.
- **Browsing for vendors does NOT require an account** — anyone can search and view vendors freely. The account is only needed to create/manage an event page (and, later, to save favourites).
- **Clients can sign in with Google and social media accounts** for fast, frictionless sign-up — alongside vendors.
